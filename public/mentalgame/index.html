04 About
" The music is the message. The mental game is what gets you there.

Mental Game is a platform built at the intersection of music and mental health. Created by Evan J, the show features raw, honest conversations with music creatives — artists, producers, songwriters — exploring how they navigate the mental and emotional demands of a life in music.

Every episode is a window into the real journey behind the art: the grind, the growth, and the mental game it takes to keep going.
